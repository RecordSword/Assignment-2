About Me


I'm an English born Australian living life in the Pilbara. I've been working on the Karratha Gas Plant for the past three years as a Coordinator for an Indigenous Electrical company. Many of my passions are mainly putting myself out of my comfort zone. I love to go out and camp in the elements of the world and go onstage with my voice and a guitar to entertain a crowd. I have a firm belief that if you are not taking a step forward in an aspect of your life, then you are living in a rut. We need to keep pushing ourselves to be a better version of who we were yesterday (or the day before if that was a rest day)
